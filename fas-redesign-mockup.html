Three Generations. One Standard.

Built by Racing.
Carried by Family.

F.A.S. Motorsports was built on a foundation most shops can't claim — a professional racing legacy started by Frank Mingione II, who set the standard before handing it to the next generation.

When Frank II passed in 2011, Frank III carried the torch. Then Frank IV brought the family's obsession with quality into a full-scale manufacturing operation in Punta Gorda, Florida. The track at Bradenton isn't just nearby — it's where our parts get proven before they ever reach a customer.

Since 2002, we've grown from a performance shop into a billet parts manufacturer with our own CNC equipment, our own fabrication team, and a B2B wholesale network serving shops across the country. Every product in our catalog exists because we needed it, couldn't find it built right, and decided to build it ourselves.

The Foundation
Frank Mingione II

Professional racer. Standard-setter. Built the culture of precision and authenticity that still defines FAS. Passed in 2011. The philosophy he established runs through everything we build.

The Bridge
Frank Mingione III

Carried the legacy after 2011. Kept the standards, expanded the operation, and made sure the family's reputation wasn't just maintained — it was built on.

Frank Mingione IV
